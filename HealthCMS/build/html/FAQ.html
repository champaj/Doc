Quick Guide¶

Q1. How to create Users like Admin, Content_Manager, Content_Creator, Doctors and Users ?

Ans. To Create users

  1. Login to ERP Click on Manage users from menu side bar
  2. Click on New then Creating New Users

Q2. How to Edit Users ?

Ans. To Edit User’s click on Manage users > User Type (As per your requirement) for Admin, Content_Manager, Content_Creator, Doctors and User Editing User’s Details

Q3. How to Manage the permissions for Users?

Ans. To Edit Permissions for Users click on Manage users > User Type (As per your requirement) for Admin, Content_Manager, Content_Creator, Doctors and User then click on their Key icon as we have done in Editing Permissions for Users

Q4. How to edit Master Data* of users?

Ans. Home > Manage Users > Click on that user’s lock Icon then follow the same as we have done in Editing Master Data for Users

Q5. How to View Profile of users ?

Ans. To view user’s profile go to Manage users > User(Choose the user type and user) > View Profile which will look like View User’s profile

Q6. How to manage Articles ?

Ans. To Manage the article follow given steps of Articles

Q7. How to Create Articles ?

Ans. To create article Go to Articles and follow the process of Creating a New Article

Q8. How to Preview the created articles?

Ans. To preview the articles go to ERP > Article > Search and filter > Click on preview icon of that article which you want to see. You will see something like Preview Article

Q9. How to Add Subcategory in Articles ?

Ans. To add subcategory Go to ERP > Article > Search and filter > Click on Edit icon of that article which you want to edit and follow the process of Editing Article

Q10. How to Publish Article ?

Ans. To publish article go to ERP > Article > Search and filter > Click on Publish icon of that article which you want to Publish and follow the process of Publishing Article but that article should be approved by content manager else send it for approval.

Q11. How to Create Categories of disease?

Ans. To create the categories go to ERP > Categories > New+ then follow the process of Creating Category

Q11. How to Edit Categories of disease?

Ans. To edit the category go to ERP > Categories > Search and select > click on Edit icon then follow the process of Editing Category

Q12. How to Create Testimonials for website?

Ans. To Create testimonials go to ERP > Testimonials > click on New+ then follow the process of Creating Testimonials

Q13. How to Edit Testimonials ?

Ans. To Edit Testimonials go to ERP > Testimonials > Search and select > Click on Edit icon of that testimonial which you want to edit and follow the process of Editing Testimonials

Q14. How to Delete Testimonials ?

Ans. To Delete Testimonials go to ERP > Testimonials > Search and select > Click on Delete icon of that testimonial which you want to delete and click on yes when it is asking for confirmation else click on no .

Q15. How to Create News for website?

Ans. To Create News go to ERP > News > click on New+ then follow the process of Creating News

Q16. How to Edit News ?

Ans. To Edit News go to ERP > News > Search and select > click on Edit (pencil) icon of that news whom you want to edit then follow the process of Editing News

Q17. How to Delete News ?

Ans. To Delete Testimonials go to ERP > News > Search and select > Click on Delete icon of that news which you want to delete and click on yes when it is asking for confirmation to delete else click on no .

Q18. How to Create Frequent Question ?

Ans. To Create Frequent Question go to ERP > Frequent Question > click on New+ then follow the process of Creating Frequent questions

Q19. How to Edit Frequent Question ?

Ans. To Edit Frequent Question go to ERP > Frequent Question > Search and select > click on Edit (pencil) icon of that Frequent Question whom you want to edit then follow the process of Editing Frequent questions

Q20. How to Delete Frequent Question ?

Ans. To Delete Frequent Question go to ERP > Frequent Question > Search > Click on Delete icon of that Frequent Question which you want to delete and click on yes when it is asking for confirmation to delete else click on no .

Q21. How to Create Appointments ?

Ans. To Create Appointments go to ERP > Appointments > click on New+ then follow the process of Creating Appointments

Q22. How to Create Job-openings ?

Ans. To Create Job-openings go to ERP > Job-openings > click on New+ then follow the process of Creating Jobopenings

Q23. How to Edit Job-openings ?

Ans. To Edit Job-openings go to ERP > Job-openings > Search and select > click on Edit (pencil) icon of that Job-openings which you want to edit then follow the process of Editing Jobopenings

Q24. How to Delete Job-openings ?

Ans. To Delete Job-openings go to ERP > Job-openings > Search > Click on Delete icon of that Job-openings which you want to delete and click on yes when it is asking for confirmation to delete else click on no .

Q25. How to Create Survey ?

Ans. To Create Survey go to ERP > Survey > click on New+ then follow the process of Creating Survey

Q26. How to Edit Survey ?

Ans. To Edit Survey go to ERP > Survey > Search and select > click on Edit (pencil) icon of that Survey which you want to edit then follow the process of Editing Survey

Q27. How to Delete Survey ?

Ans. To Delete Survey go to ERP > Survey > Search > Click on Delete icon of that Survey which you want to delete and click on yes when it is asking for confirmation to delete else click on no .
